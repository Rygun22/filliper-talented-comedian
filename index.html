Filliper Wanjiru,10,from Bahati North Academy, is a content creator that has taken the internet by storm.She shares the interesting story of how she discovered her talentand how she is nurturing it.
  • Take us through your process generating content

First my aunt helps me look for content ideas. I then set up my video shooting equipments.I use a tripod ,phone and ringlight to shoot the clips.I alsoadd costumes and props to make the scenes realistic .For instance, if am doing african parents character. I usea leso,kitamba and a sweater as a costume. After I dress , I memorise my lines , then we atart shooting.

  • Out of all your videos, do you have a favorite?

Yes the clip where I was lip syncing to Star Chebets comedy. I liked how she was doing her content and we decided to shoot it. The video got a lot of likes and it increased my followers.

First you need to patient becasuse content ideas do not always turn out as expected.Consistency is also important.The more you post consistently the more you get views and likes.
